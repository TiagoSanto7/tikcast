# Privacy Policy - TikCast

1. **Data Collection**: We only access TikTok data (user info, video lists) that you explicitly authorize via OAuth.
2. **Data Usage**: Data is used solely to execute the workflows you create in your n8n instance.
3. **Data Storage**: We do not store your TikTok credentials on our servers; they are managed by your n8n installation.
4. **Third Parties**: We do not share your information with third parties.
